Mitigating Coperate Information Exposure On The Web

Data leakage is defined as the procedure of which data is unintentionally transferred from an individual visiting a third party website.(Bertrand Bathelot, December 2012, Page 1). Data leakage is split into two categories(Amin Babiker, April 2015, Page 23); Intentional and Unintentional Data leakage. Unintentional Data Leakage occurs due to miscalculations and mistakes by employees, more often than usual this is occurring in new employees during training phases. However, Intentional Data leakage tends to occur when a company targets an individual who works there. This may lead to phishing data leakage.(Sharifa Hassan, April 2015, Pages 23-25) Read More...
